Climate Crisis

I chose this advocacy because I believe that it is an urgent global issue that endangers all life on this planet. Because of this, I think that we should all contribute to slowing down the effects of the climate crisis. This is not an issue that can be solved by an individual or group of people only. As such, spreading awareness about the topic is vital to ending this crisis.

In order to ensure that people will act, it is imperative that they know the factors that contribute towards worsening the issue at hand. It is also essential to inform them about the effects that this crisis brought about. We must all hep each other to solve this climate crisis that threatens all of us.

How YOU Can Help!

Saving the Earth alone is impossible. We all need to work together to stop this crisis. Reduce the use of plastics by purchasing reusable items. Reduce carbon footprint. Save electricity whenever you can.

Spread awareness to more people. Protest against the usage of fossil fuels. Join activities such as clean-up drives and tree planting activities. Any amount of help is progress towards our goal.
